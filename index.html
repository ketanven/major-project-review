Silver Oak College of Computer Application

Freelancer Work Tracking &
Invoice Management System

A comprehensive web-based solution for managing freelance work, tracking time, and generating invoices.

01 // Problem Statement

The Freelancer's Dilemma

  • Difficulty tracking multiple projects

    Juggling various client scopes leading to missed deadlines and scope creep.

  • Manual invoicing

    Time-consuming creation of invoices resulting in math errors and delayed payments.

  • Poor time tracking

    Inaccurate logging of billable hours causing freelancers to lose earned revenue.

  • Disorganized client management

    Scattered communication and details lead to unprofessional client experiences.

02 // Project Objective

System Goals

To build a unified platform that eliminates administrative overhead, allowing freelancers to focus entirely on their craft and client delivery.

01

Simplify freelance work tracking

02

Automate invoice generation

03

Manage projects and clients efficiently

04

Provide a centralized analytical dashboard

03 // System Overview

How It Works

The system acts as a central hub bridging execution and administration.

It helps freelancers manage incoming tasks, track the exact time spent on them using built-in timers, and subsequently pull that productivity data directly into professional invoices within one unified platform.

04 // Capabilities

Key Features

Work Tracking

Create, assign, and monitor the status of individual project tasks seamlessly.

Time Tracking

Start and stop timers per task to precisely log billable and non-billable hours.

Invoice Generation

Automatically convert logged hours into professional, itemized PDF invoices.

Client Management

Maintain a secure directory of client contacts, hourly rates, and billing preferences.

Dashboard Analytics

Visualize total earnings, outstanding payments, and productivity trends over time.
